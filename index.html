about us

Two sisters.
One daughter.
She draws everything.

Piel (Spanish: skin) is a family-made temporary tattoo brand. We're two sisters, two years apart, and our 12-year-old niece whose sketchbook is the source of every single design.

Florida born. Hispanic. Living between cultures — the brand carries that everywhere it goes. One syllable, two languages. Insiders recognize it instantly. Everyone else finds it short, beautiful, and slightly mysterious.

Every design hides something. Find it.

Piel Libre The core collection. Quarterly rotations from the sketchbook.
Piel Habla Skin speaks. Functional tattoos for nonverbal kids, AAC users, and Deaf families.
Piel Edición Seasonal cultural drops. Día de los Muertos. Nochebuena. Quinceañera season.
